Privacy Policy

Application: TBM Fleet Manager
Operated by: TBM & Associates Pty Ltd
Last updated: 17 April 2026

1. Overview

TBM Fleet Manager ("the Application") is an internal business tool operated by TBM & Associates Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our"). It is used solely for managing vehicle rental invoices and is not available to the general public. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle data accessed through the QuickBooks Online API.

2. Data We Access

Through the QuickBooks Online API, the Application may access:

  • Customer names and contact information stored in QuickBooks
  • Invoice records, line items, and payment history
  • Company and account information

3. How We Use the Data

Data accessed through the QuickBooks API is used exclusively to:

  • Create and update rental invoices for drivers
  • View existing invoice balances and payment status
  • Manage internal fleet rental records

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose any data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

The Application does not maintain its own database. All financial data remains stored within QuickBooks Online. No customer data is stored externally or on third-party servers outside of Intuit's platform.

5. Data Security

Access to the Application is restricted to authorised personnel of TBM & Associates Pty Ltd. OAuth 2.0 tokens used to authenticate with QuickBooks are stored securely and are never shared.

6. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with QuickBooks Online, operated by Intuit Inc. Use of QuickBooks is subject to Intuit's own Privacy Policy, available at https://www.intuit.com/privacy/statement/.

7. Your Rights

As this is an internal business application, data access and management rights are governed by TBM & Associates Pty Ltd's internal policies. For any privacy-related enquiries, please contact us directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.

9. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact TBM & Associates Pty Ltd.
